# Georgia Voter Guide — What It Is & Why It Matters
### A plain-language summary to share with friends, family, and coworkers

---

## The 30-Second Version

Georgia just made it harder to know who you're voting for. Party labels have been
removed from ballots, and district maps are being redrawn right now. 

This free tool fixes that. You type in a candidate's name or your home address,
and it tells you everything you need to know before you vote — including party
affiliation, policy positions, your polling place, and whether your district
changed.

It's free, nonpartisan, and built right here in Georgia.

---

## Why This Was Built

Three things happened in Georgia that motivated this project:

**1. Party labels removed from ballots**
The governor signed a bill removing party affiliation from Georgia ballots.
That means when you walk in to vote, you'll see a list of names — and nothing
else. No (D), no (R), no indication of who stands for what. This tool gives
you that information back.

**2. Redistricting special session**
A special session was called to redraw Georgia's congressional district maps.
This happens after court decisions or census changes, and it means the district
you voted in last time may no longer exist in the same form. Your representative
may have changed. Even your polling place may have moved.

**3. Supreme Court weakened the Voting Rights Act**
A 2026 Supreme Court decision made it harder for minority communities to
challenge maps drawn to dilute their votes. This tool helps every voter —
especially in affected communities — know exactly who represents them and
who is on their ballot.

## What the App Does

**Look up any candidate**
Type a name and get their party, office, district, and key policy positions.
The app pulls from live government data sources first, then uses AI to fill
in the rest — and always tells you which is which.

**Find your representatives by address**
Type your home address and see every elected official who represents you —
U.S. Congress, Georgia Senate, Georgia House — with party and contact info.

**Check if your district changed**
A dedicated tool that shows you the Georgia redistricting history, explains
what changed, and lets you look up your current district. Especially useful
right now during the special session.

**Election logistics**
Polling place location, early voting sites, ballot drop boxes, registration
deadlines, voter ID requirements — all by address, all verified against
official sources.

## Is It Partisan?

No. The app is built to be strictly nonpartisan:

- It never recommends who to vote for
- It presents both parties' information equally
- It shows factual policy positions only — no opinions
- Every result is labeled as either live verified data or AI training data
- The AI is specifically instructed to refuse any request to favor one party

The goal is simple: informed voters make better decisions. That's good for
everyone regardless of party.

---

## Is It Safe and Private?

Yes. Here's how your data is protected:

- You create an account with just an email and password
- Your search history is stored securely and only visible to you
- Your API keys and data are never exposed publicly
- Harmful queries (voter suppression, election fraud, harassment) are
  automatically blocked before they reach the AI
- Built on Supabase with row-level security — industry standard protection
